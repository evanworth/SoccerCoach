Features

Everything in one quick game-day workflow

Live game tools

Use the built-in game timer, halftime notes, and substitution windows without leaving the sideline view.

7v7 lineup planning

Set your field shape, assign players to multiple positions, save presets, and reset back to a standard 7v7 setup.

Fair-play tracking

Track minutes automatically and get sub suggestions so playing time stays more balanced across the team.

Fast roster management

Keep a simple roster with player names, jersey numbers, and playable positions for easier game-day decisions.

Saved practice plans

Build reusable sessions with drills, notes, links, and drill pictures, then store them for the season.

Secure access

Coaches sign in with Google or Apple, and the premium app experience is unlocked with an annual subscription.

Privacy at a glance

Built to keep team planning simple.
