SoccerCoach Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SoccerCoach collects, uses, and stores information when you use the SoccerCoach iPhone app.

1. Information we collect

SoccerCoach may collect or receive the following categories of information:

  • Account information, such as your name and email address, when you sign in with Google or Sign in with Apple.
  • Subscription information from Apple to verify whether you have an active annual subscription.
  • Coaching content that you create in the app, such as player names, jersey numbers, playable positions, game notes, lineups, substitution plans, and practice plans.
  • Photos you choose to attach to practice drills.

2. How information is used

SoccerCoach uses information to:

  • Authenticate your account and let you sign in.
  • Confirm your subscription status and allow access to paid features.
  • Store and display the coaching information you create in the app.
  • Support core app functions such as lineups, timer tools, substitution planning, fair-play tracking, and practice planning.

3. How your information is stored

Coaching data created in the current version of SoccerCoach is stored locally on your device. The app also stores basic account state locally so you can continue using the app after signing in.

Subscription status is checked through Apple’s in-app purchase system. Sign-in is handled through Google or Apple, depending on the method you choose.

4. Photos and media

If you attach a photo to a drill, SoccerCoach accesses only the photo or photos you choose through the iPhone photo picker. Those images are used to display your practice content inside the app.

5. Data sharing

SoccerCoach does not sell your personal information. The app relies on third-party providers only as needed to provide authentication and billing, including:

  • Google, if you choose Google sign-in.
  • Apple, if you choose Sign in with Apple or make a purchase through Apple In-App Purchase.

6. Data retention

Information you create in the app remains on your device until you delete it, remove the app, or clear the app’s local data through device-level actions.

7. Children’s privacy

SoccerCoach is intended for coaches and other adults managing a youth soccer team. The app is not directed to children as users.

8. Your choices

  • You can choose whether to sign in with Google or Apple.
  • You can edit or delete coaching information you have entered in the app.
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription through your Apple account settings.

9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
